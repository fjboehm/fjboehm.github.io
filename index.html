I hold two Masters' in Architecture, with studies in Santiago and Berlin, where I focused on design, theory, and technical approaches. I have worked in small-scale architecture studios, specializing in residential projects, renovations, and civic buildings. Additionally, I gained industry experience managing logistics during major maintenance projects in process plants.

I enjoy tackling complex challenges, solving problems, exploring meaningful design, and experimenting with new technologies.
