I hold a Master’s in Architecture, with studies in Santiago and Berlin, where I focused on design, theory, and technical approaches. I have worked in small-scale architecture studios, specializing in residential projects, renovations, and civic buildings. Additionally, I gained industry experience managing logistics during major maintenance projects in process plants.

I enjoy tackling complex challenges, thriving in problem-solving environments, exploring meaningful design, and experimenting with new technologies. While I don’t follow a defined professional path, I embrace a dynamic approach to my career, constantly exploring new opportunities and adapting to different challenges. This flexibility allows me to continuously evolve and bring fresh perspectives to my work.

I’m passionate about cooking and pastry. I love the back-and-forth process of experimenting with recipes. I also enjoy watching volleyball and try to play whenever I get the chance.
